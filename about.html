About 急救时刻

急救时刻 (Emergency Moments) is a podcast dedicated to teaching essential first aid skills to everyone. We believe that knowing basic emergency response can save lives — and that knowledge should be accessible, clear, and easy to remember.

Each episode covers one specific emergency scenario — from CPR to allergic reactions — with step-by-step guidance, memorable stories, and practical tips you can use in real situations.

Our content is designed for all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a parent, teacher, student, or just someone who wants to be prepared — we've got you covered.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This podcast is for educational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical training. In any real emergency, always call your local emergency number first (e.g., 911, 999, 112).
